PRODAO Wallet Privacy Policy Non-Custodial · Self-Sovereign

PRODAO Wallet ("the Service") is a non-custodial decentralized wallet. We do not collect, store, or control any personal information or digital assets on behalf of users. All keys, transaction data, and identifiers are managed directly by you, on your own device and on the public blockchain.

1 Overview

PRODAO Wallet ("the Service") is a non-custodial decentralized wallet that does not collect, store, or control any user personal information or assets. All keys, transaction data, and identifiers are managed entirely by the user on their own device or the public blockchain network.

2 Information We Collect

The Service does not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII). No name, email, phone number, identity document, government ID, or similar personal identifier is requested or retained.

However, limited non-personal technical data may be processed automatically for performance and security:

  • Device metadata (OS version, app version, language settings).
  • Network metadata (IP address, region, connectivity status).
  • Blockchain transaction records (public on-chain data only).

These technical data points are not linked to any identifiable individual and are used solely for service stability and security auditing.

3 Purpose of Use

The limited technical data described above are used only for:

  • Service performance monitoring and debugging.
  • Security and abuse prevention (including fraud, money laundering, and sanctions evasion monitoring).
  • Transaction verification within the blockchain network (e.g. swap, send, receive flows).

4 Data Storage & Retention

No personal information is stored on any centralized server. All transaction and key-related data remain solely on the user's device and/or the blockchain network.

Private keys are generated, encrypted, and stored locally on the user's device. They are never transmitted to, synchronized with, or backed up on PRODAO Wallet's servers.

Temporary operational logs (if generated) are kept only for security, diagnostics, or abuse prevention, and are deleted once no longer necessary for those purposes.

5 Third-Party Disclosure

PRODAO Wallet does not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties, and does not disclose personal information because it does not collect such data in the first place.

In rare cases, anonymized and aggregated technical statistics (for example, crash frequency or general performance analytics) may be used internally to improve service quality. These statistics cannot be used to identify an individual.

We may disclose information if required by applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request related to fraud prevention, investigation, or enforcement.

6 International Data Transfer

Because PRODAO Wallet does not process or store personal information on centralized servers, there is no "international transfer of personal data" in the conventional regulatory sense.

By design, blockchain networks operate on globally distributed public ledgers. Public on-chain transaction data may be replicated worldwide across independent nodes. Such on-chain data generally do not constitute personally identifiable information.

7 Security Measures

We apply industry-standard security measures intended to protect the Service, including:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmissions (for example, ECDH-based secure channels or equivalent cryptographic protections).
  • Local-only private key management. Private keys and seed phrases are generated, encrypted, and stored exclusively on your device.
  • No key escrow. We do not retain recovery copies of your private keys. You are solely responsible for backup and safekeeping of your keys and seed.

Important: Loss of your private key or seed phrase may result in permanent loss of access to your assets. Because PRODAO Wallet is non-custodial, we cannot restore or reissue your private key.

8 Children's Privacy

PRODAO Wallet is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13 years old.
